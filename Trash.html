Trash/Recycle How-To

Location

The trash and recycle bins are located under the breezeway, next to the garage.

Schedule

During the summer season, trash/recycle pick up is WEDNESDAYS and SATURDAYS. In order for the trash to be picked up, you need to take the cans down to the end of the driveways by Wednesday and Saturday morning. After the trash/recycle has been picked up, they will return the cans to under the carport.
